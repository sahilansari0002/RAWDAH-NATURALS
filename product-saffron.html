Saffron Night Cream

Wake Up to Healthy, Soft & Glowing Skin

Directions for Use

How to Use Saffron Night Cream

1
Clean your face before bedtime.
2
Apply a small amount evenly over the face and neck.
3
Massage gently until absorbed.
4
Leave overnight — let it work while you sleep.
5
Wash your face in the morning as usual.
📦 Storage
  • Store in a cool and dry place
  • Away from direct sunlight
  • Keep tightly closed when not in use
⚠️ Safety Information
  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs
