Rooted in Moroccan Tradition

Moroccan Blue Indigo (Nila) has been used for centuries in traditional North African skincare. Known for its purifying and brightening properties, it is a treasured ingredient in traditional beauty rituals.

Directions for Use

How to Use Moroccan Blue Nila Soap

1
Wet your skin with water.
2
Apply the soap gently to your skin.
3
Massage for 1–2 minutes in circular motions.
4
Rinse thoroughly with water.
5
Use daily for best results.
📦 Storage
  • Store in a cool and dry place
  • Away from direct sunlight
  • Allow soap to dry between uses
⚠️ Safety Information
  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Stop use if irritation develops
  • Keep out of children's reach
